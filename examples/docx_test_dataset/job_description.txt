JUNIOR DATA ANALYST

We are looking for a Junior Data Analyst who can turn business data into clear,
accurate insights for operational and management decisions.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES

- Clean, transform and analyse datasets using Python and pandas.
- Write SQL queries to retrieve and combine relational data.
- Build reports and dashboards using Microsoft Excel and Power BI.
- Create clear data visualizations and explain important trends.
- Apply basic statistics to check patterns and data quality.
- Use Git to maintain analysis files and collaborate with teammates.
- Document data definitions, assumptions and analytical findings.

REQUIRED SKILLS

Python, SQL, Microsoft Excel, Power BI, pandas, Data Visualization, Statistics, Git

PREFERRED SKILLS

Tableau, NumPy, scikit-learn

EXPERIENCE

At least 2 years of relevant data analysis, reporting or business intelligence
experience. Internships and substantial project work may support junior candidates.

EDUCATION

Bachelor's degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science, Economics,
Business Analytics or a related quantitative field.
